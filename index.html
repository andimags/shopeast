About Us

We are back Warriors! USC has launched its project ShopEast last June to help the UE students who are into online selling to help their family during quarantine. Now, we are taking it back for the Warrior entrepreneurs who are now selling products as part of their requirement for their subject this semester.

Registration is until October 17, 2020 - 12 PM NOTE: Aside from BSBA Financial Management, BSBA Marketing Management and BSBA Business Management, this is also open to UE senior highschool and other non-business major courses who have to sell products for their subject requirement this semester. For inquiry message USC Business Manager Charrie Ever Juan. Happy Shopping, Warriors!
